Remix Digital Collage

This is my work for digital collage. The tools I used for this work Adobe Photoshop. My inspiration comes from news of the situation in the white house. The concept is base on the reflection of the United State. As you can see, the country, or government, is slowly crumbling along with internal and external conflicts. Some of the symbolisms you might recognizes that represent the current conflict with our nation.
