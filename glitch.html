Glitch Art

The tools I used for this art were Audacity and NotePadplus along with Photoshop timeline. I messed around settings in Audacity and mixed up some codes with the text-editor to reach the results. My inspiration for this work was exploring how we use technology. Sometimes we make an accidental discovery by misusing the wrong tools. I do not create any concept or meaning to this because it just to play around with.
