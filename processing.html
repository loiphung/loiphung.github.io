Processing

Self Portrait

Movie made by Processing.

These works were made with a program called Processing. The portrait consists of multiple ellicpses, rectangles, and arcs to create a human. It does not look great I will be honest because I am lazy. The bouncing ball was made by filling in voids with mouseX and mouseY. Same goes for fill and the size of the ball. You may already notice that there is interactive background of the website. That is an integrated javascript following all your movements of your mouse. If you clicked, it will change to an rectangle. If you press and hold, the color will change except for the stroke.
