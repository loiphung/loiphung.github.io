Video and Sound project

The tools used for this project were an Iphone 6s and Adobe Premiere. The inspiration for this was the recent school shooting in Feburary. The concept is about a cycle; the first stage is the occurance of mass shooting. Next, you have the mourning stage. Lastly, you have a debate over gun control. This cycle has been repeated over and over for every massive school shooting like Sandy Hook.
